Instruction

On the easy level this is the classic snake game. You can control the snake by arrows on the keyboard.

One fruit gives you one point. Every 10 points the snake will be faster.

On the hard level you can turn on additional options in settings.

Bombs - eating a bomb removes 3 points. The bomb will show once every half a minute for a few seconds.

Blocked walls - going through the walls ends the game.

Random obstacles - at the beginning of the game 10 random obstacles will be added to the board, hitting them ends the game.

Scores

Chart shows last 10 scores (including the best result) with upper line for max score. Color of the point indicates what color the snake was during the game.
