Instruction

On the easy level this is the classic snake game. You can control the snake by arrows on the keyboard.

One fruit gives you one point. Every 10 points the snake will be faster.

On the hard level you can turn on additional options in settings.

Bombs - eating a bomb removes 5 points. The bombs will show once a minute.

Blocked walls - the attempt at going through the walls ends the game.

Random obstacles - at the beginning of the game random obstacles will be added to the board. Hitting them ends the game.
